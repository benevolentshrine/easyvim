# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in EasyVim a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Being respectful and inclusive
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what's best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or imagery
- Publishing others' private information

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported by opening an issue. Maintainers will review and respond appropriately.
